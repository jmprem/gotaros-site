What TAROS is

TAROS is the safety and governance layer for AI systems.

It sits between powerful AI models, agents, tools, and real-world actions—so intelligent systems can operate with control, accountability, and trust.

Policies

Define what AI systems may, must, or must not do.

Decisions

Why it is needed

The AI era needs more than intelligence. It needs guarded intelligence.

As AI moves from chat to action, the risks multiply: autonomous decisions, unpredictable behavior, compliance pressure, security exposure, and fragile multi-agent workflows.

Without an explicit control layer, capability can outrun safety.
